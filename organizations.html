Philippine Tourism Organizations

  • Department of Tourism (DOT) - The main agency promoting Philippine tourism.
  • Tourism Promotions Board (TPB) - Focuses on marketing and promotions.
  • Philippine Tour Operators Association (PHILTOA) - Represents tour operators across the country.
  • Hotel and Restaurant Association of the Philippines (HRAP) - Supports the hospitality industry.
  • National Parks Development Committee (NPDC) - Manages parks like Rizal Park.
